WHY? --- class: center, middle ![:scale 100%]({{ site.baseurl}}/assets/images/20191022_pillars_mission_codingclub.png) --- class: center, middle ## Mission The INBO Coding Club unites all ecologists, researchers and coders who want to develop their (R) programming skills in a pleasant and supportive environment in order to replace the fear for coding with inspiration and motivation to learn. --- ## Expect this - How to read the data with such awkward text format provided by OCOSA (Office for Complication of Otherwise Simple Affairs)? - What is the purpose of the `%>%` operator? - How can I JOIN two tables in R? - How to convert some scattered code into a reusable R function? - ... --- class: left, middle ## Do NOT expect this - Is a GLM the proper choice for my experiment? - How to implement a species distribution model? - What is machine learning? --- class: center, middle ![:scale 70%]({{ site.baseurl}}/assets/images/20191022_history_sessions_codingclub.png)


[Coding club sessions info](https://inbo.github.io/coding-club/sessions/index.html) --- class: left, middle ## So, practically... The coding club is held monthly on alternate Tuesday and Thursday, from 10 a.m. to 12 a.m., in Herman Terilinck building (Tour & Taxis, Brussels).
![:scale 70%]({{ site.baseurl}}/assets/images/20191022_building_and_room.png) --- class: left, middle ## So, practically... - The coding club is typically limited to 25 participants (room capacity). It will increase soon to 40 in 2020... - Each session focusses on a specific theme and is independent from previous sessions - No laptop, no party. We code, after all! --- class: center, middle ## Who organises the coding club? Five people, the dream team ![:scale 70%]({{ site.baseurl}}/assets/images/20191022_codingclub_team.png) --- class: left, middle ## Tasks The core team takes care of: - reservation of the lecture room - thematic topics, working material and some challenges to work on - general agenda, __taking into account the feedback of the participants__ More details: Check [this page](https://inbo.github.io/coding-club/workflow.html) to see the step-by-step workflow to organize an INBO coding club. --- class: left, middle ## Did we figure that out ourselves?
