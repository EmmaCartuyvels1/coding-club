Fill the provinces with a color based on their area (column `SURFACE.GIS.km2`) and provide a nice title to the legend, i.e. "Area (km2)". Hint:[building_maps_in_layers](https://workshop.mhermans.net/thematic-maps-r/04_plot.html#building_maps_in_layers). - Divide them by region using facets. Hint: [multiple_maps__facets](https://workshop.mhermans.net/thematic-maps-r/04_plot.html#multiple_maps__facets). You could end up with something like [this]({{ site.baseurl}}/assets/images/20200428_example_provinces_tmap.html). --- background-image: url({{ site.baseurl}}/assets/images/background_challenge_2.png) class: left, middle # Challenge 2 Starting from the [`20200428_challenges.R`](https://github.com/inbo/coding-club/blob/master/src/20200428_challenges.R) file: - Generate a *leaflet* map centered at lng 4.89 and lat 51.01 with zoom level 7. Hint: https://rstudio.github.io/leaflet/basemaps.html - Add markers at butterfly observations coordinates (`decimal_longitude`, `decimal_latitude`). Cluster them using the cluster option. Hint: https://rstudio.github.io/leaflet/markers.html - Add pop-ups to the markers showing the date, e.g. "Date: 2015-09-20". Hint: https://rstudio.github.io/leaflet/popups.html - Add also labels to the markers showing the species, e.g. "Atalanta". ![:scale 70%]({{ site.baseurl}}/assets/images/20200428_example_butterflies_markers.png) --- background-image: url({{ site.baseurl}}/assets/images/background_challenge_3.png) class: left, middle # Challenge 3 Starting from the [`20200428_challenges.R`](https://github.com/inbo/coding-club/blob/master/src/20200428_challenges.R) file: - Make a choropleth map of belgian provinces based on their area (column `SURFACE.GIS.km2`). Provide a legend as well. Hint: https://rstudio.github.io/leaflet/choropleths.html - Add markers at the centroids of the provinces. Add labels using the Dutch name of the provinces (column `TX_PROV_DESCR_NL`). ![:scale 70%]({{ site.baseurl}}/assets/images/20200428_example_provinces_choropleths.png) --- class: left, middle # Bonus challenge If you didn't have enough, then you earn a bonus!
